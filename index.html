Projects

The SCG Project

The Second Chance Gap Project aims to improve the rate at which eligible offenders apply for, and are granted, criminal record expungement, meaning their crimes are removed from the public record.

Our team found that only about 5% of eligible persons in surveyed jurisdictions have taken advantage of this service, meaning they are being negatively affected when applying for loans and jobs for no reason.

This inspired our team to create a web application that allows offenders to answer a series of questions and check if they are eligible for the expungement program within their state.

Yang Near Me

Built a ReactJS web application that displayed volunteer events across the country to support Andrew Yang's 2020 Presidential Campaign.

These events were aggregated from multiple online sources, including mobilize.us and Facebook.

Reduced average volunteer travel distance by 27% by suggesting more centrally located areas to hold events and contacting leaders in said region.
